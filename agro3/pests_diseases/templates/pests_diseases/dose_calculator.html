Safety Tips
  • Always wear protective equipment when handling chemicals
  • Follow the manufacturer's instructions on the chemical label
  • Mix chemicals in well-ventilated areas
  • Keep chemicals away from children and animals
  • Dispose of empty containers properly
  • If in doubt, consult with agricultural experts
